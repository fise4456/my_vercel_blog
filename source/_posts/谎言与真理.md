##  Lies And Truth

Lies are the demand of society.
They are like noise-canceling earmuffs, silencing the voice of truth.
A cloth over the eyes, blinding the way forward.
A dream-making machine, feeding illusions to the restless crowd.
To believe a lie requires no thought.
But to believe the truth demands rare insight and deep ingenuity.
In such a world, truth remains forever weaker than lies.

##  谎言与真理

谎言是社会的需求。
它们就像降噪耳罩，封住了真理的声音；
像蒙眼的布，遮住了前行的道路；
像造梦的机器，向躁动的人群输送幻象。
相信谎言无需思考，
而相信真理却需要稀有的洞察力与深刻的智慧。
在这样的世界里，真理永远比谎言更加脆弱。
